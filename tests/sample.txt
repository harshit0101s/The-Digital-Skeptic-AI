The Ministry said inflation would likely fall to 4% by Q3 2025, according to a briefing note shared on Tuesday.
However, several analysts warned that food prices could remain elevated. The report cited figures from last month's consumer index but did not link to the underlying dataset.
One senior official, who asked to remain anonymous, said the central bank was prepared to adjust policy if needed.
Critics argue the outlook is too optimistic and fails to consider the effects of export restrictions.
